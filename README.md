## 💡 The Story

I was studying for my NLP exam and had a bunch of topics left — speech sounds, phonemes, IPA, phonological rules, and how all of this connects to NLP systems. Instead of just reading slides, I thought why not make something interactive and actually fun to study from?

So I used **Claude AI** to help me build this. I discussed the concepts, structured the content, and iterated on the design — all while learning the material myself. End result: I understood the topic _and_ got a cool project out of it.

---

## ✨ What's Inside

- **Speech Sounds** — consonants vs vowels, voiced/voiceless pairs, minimal pairs
- **Phoneme & IPA** — clickable IPA chart with 24 consonants + 12 vowels, phoneme vs allophone visual
- **Phonological Rules** — assimilation, deletion, flapping, vowel reduction + formal notation
- **NLP Applications** — G2P pipelines, Soundex algorithm, MFCCs, real Python code snippets
- **Quiz** — 6 interactive questions with instant feedback and progress tracking

---

## 🎨 Design

Dark theme, animated grid background, color-coded cards, smooth tab transitions. Pure HTML/CSS/JS — no frameworks, no build step, no dependencies. Just one file.
